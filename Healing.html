Healing


Healers cast spells to refill the health point of all players in the raid and when things go bad they can also bring players back to life. For the most part healers are also responsible for providing buffs to increase players stats and some can cast spells to help mitigate damage. Each raid usually has around 10 healers and each raid aims to have at least one of every healing class they can.


Healing Classes


Priest Great tank healer that is strong in solo target healing. Has additional abilities to help tanks mitigate damage and a buff that all raids need.
Druid Uses healing over time abilities and does not keep up with priests for solo target heals. Has a strong buff ability and the only healer that can resurrect players during combat making them essential for a raid.
Paladin Good solo target healing and can use multiple strong buff abilities. Has less problems with resource management then priests.
Shaman Strong at solo and group healing. Uses totems that are very important for raids. Can have serious resource management issues but they are always in high demand.
