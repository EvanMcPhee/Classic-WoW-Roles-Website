Tanking


As a tank your job is to protect your teammates from taking damage. You are up front with the bad guys in close range combat keeping their attention on you (keeping threat) so your healers only have one person to worry about. Tanks focus on keeping threat and mitigating damage towards them. Keep in mind there are usually only 4 or 5 tanks in a raid but for the most part they are in high demand.


Tanking classes


Warrior Strongest tank in the game that uses two melee weapons or one with a shield. Wears plate armor and has many damage mitigation abilities. Almost every group will have a warrior as the main tank.
Druid Can play as a tank using bear form. Difficult to get the right armor for but not bad for holding threat. Does not have many options to mitigate damage.
Paladin Good for holding threat on multiple targets but difficult to hold threat on single targets. Resource management from using mana and lack of a taunt makes paladin a rather weak tank and they are usually off tanks.
Shaman Similar to a paladin Shaman tanks have difficulties holding threat on single target fights and they also need to manage mana usage. Shaman tanks are not usually the main tank but are viable off tanks
