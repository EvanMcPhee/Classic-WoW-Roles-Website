Damage


Every class in the game can play the role of a damage dealer but there are some that are widely considered better then others. The below table lists if a class is ranged or melee combat type and some more information on the class itself. In most raid groups there will be a pretty even split of ranged and melee damage classes usually leaning more on melee.


Damage Classes


Warrior Melee Strongest melee class but very gear and consumable dependent. Difficulties staying behind tank in aggro but with late game gear good survivability.
Warlock Ranged Very strong ranged damage. Has serious issue controlling threat but comes with multiple abilities that benefit the raid.
Rogue Melee Good melee damage but can be difficult to gear with a shortage of weapons. Has abilities to help reduce threat which increases damage output.
Hunter Ranged Weak ranged damage but has useful utility abilities and buffs. Most raids require at least 2 hunters for different bosses.
Priest Ranged Can do damage if using shadow talents. Weak damage overall and each raid can only use one shadow priest.
Druid Melee/Ranged Can do damage in cat form or with spells. Weak damage for both makes druid a low tier choice for dealing damage.
Paladin Melee Weak melee damage but usefull for buffs they can provide. Issues with resource management and they require the same gear as warriors making them not desirable for most raids.
Shaman Melee Strong melee damage but falls off in longer fights due to limited resources. Uses totems that are helpful to raid but most shamans in a raid will be healers.
